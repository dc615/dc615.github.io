-- 32Gb MicroSD card: https://goo.gl/I9F199

-- Controller 2 pack: https://goo.gl/0AlWmJ

Tips & Resources:

- Make sure the video source is turned on before powering up the Pi.

- You have to optimize N64 roms to get them to work correctly.

- Remove black border (Overscan) on your display and overclock the Pi:
